OUR DARK HISTORY

Founded in 1950 by the enigmatic Mr. Bartholomew, our circus began as a traveling showcase of medical oddities and supernatural phenomena. What started as a collection of strange specimens quickly evolved into the most popular and amazing show on earth thanks to the Mr Charlie that gave us a better way for our shows in the front yard of his Chocolate Factory

It all began as a series of small shows back in the 1950s, but it grew in popularity until the tragic accident that changed everything. Those mistakes are part of the past now. The place has been fully restored — and it’s more spectacular than ever.

In 1980, it reopened its gates to a new generation, with more attractions, more mascots, and more people dedicated to giving you the time of your life.

The staff is incredible — a team of passionate performers and creators who are determined to make your visit a one-of-a-kind experience you’ll never forget.

Our current lineup includes daring performers, wild beasts, a fortune teller who can read your future in an instant, magicians who vanish and reappear before your eyes, beast tamers, and many more surprises just waiting to be discovered.

We travel in a convoy of 50 black caravans… and we’re inviting you to join us at the greatest show in history — where you become part of the performance.
