Hamer

The Super Power


    A challenge for the bold. A test of pure strength.
Here, only the mightiest dare to face The Hammer — a towering device that has humbled countless hopefuls.
Many have tried. Most have failed.

But two years ago, something changed.
A mysterious guest stepped forward, gripped the hammer, and—
with a single strike — rang the bell at the top.

No one knows who they were.
All anyone remembers… is the sound that echoed through the carnival.
