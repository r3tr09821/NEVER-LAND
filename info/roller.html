Mr. Jonathan’s Roller Coaster


    A ride many have dared to try, but few have returned for a second go.
With dizzying speed and impossible turns, it’s said to steal the breath—and courage—of even the bravest souls.
Its enigmatic host, Mr. Jonathan, is a carnival veteran, present long before the Carnival War was ever written in history.
One of the first to serve under the big top, and a loyal companion to the elusive Mr. Charlie, he watches the ride with a knowing smile... as if he knows something the rest of us don't.
