The Games of Wacky Willy


    A domain ruled by Mr. Willy, where legend has it that no one has ever won — not a single game.
Some whisper that Willy cheats, rigging the odds in his favor. Others swear there’s a curse that dooms every challenger to failure.
But many don’t even try anymore. His fierce temper keeps most away, and the few brave souls who dare speak of his aggressive nature.

Last Halloween, a heated brawl broke out between Willy and a challenger—
but despite his wild spirit, Willy was bested by a far stronger opponent.
Now, the air around his games feels even more charged, as if the legend itself is watching, waiting.
